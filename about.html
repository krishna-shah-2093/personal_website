Bio

Hi, I'm Krishna. I'm from Ahmedabad, India, now pursuing my MSIS at IU Kelley after an undergrad in Computer Science & Design. I have a knack for tech, a keen attention to detail, I'm punctual, and I love to collaborate. With a foundation in computing and design—and growing business training—I'm able to see the big picture and find the sweet spot between people, business, and technology.

Outside work and school, I'm usually with friends, spending far too long cracking board games (very competitive), exploring new cafés, catching live music, or chasing sunsets at the beach. I'm always up for an adventure—call it 'experience the experience.'

Goals: Grow into a Tech Business Analyst—the connector who turns real user needs into clear requirements, aligns data with decisions, and helps teams ship the right thing. Lead cross-functional efforts where solid process and thoughtful product meet, while creating spaces where more women feel at home in tech.
